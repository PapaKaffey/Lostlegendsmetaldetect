Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need permission?

Yes. You must have property owner permission before searching. For public areas, follow local rules.

How long does a search take?

Most recoveries take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on conditions, target size, and search area.

How does pricing work?

We confirm travel/gas up front. For eligible ring/jewelry recoveries, we may offer no find, no fee (labor). If recovered, you can tip/pay whatever you feel is fair (many clients choose $50–$200+). Larger or uncertain-area searches may use a flat or hourly plan agreed before starting.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Southeast Nebraska and Southwest Iowa. Travel outside the core area may be available by request.

What is no find, no fee?

For eligible recoveries, you only pay for labor if the item is found. This is usually offered for ring/jewelry recoveries when: (1) you have permission, (2) the drop zone is reasonably defined, and (3) conditions are safe to search. If the area is very large or the location is unknown, we’ll agree on a flat or hourly plan up front.

Do I keep what’s found during relic searches?

Yes. For relic/farmstead searches, you keep everything found unless you ask us to haul away scrap/junk metal.
