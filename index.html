Frequently asked questions

Do I need permission?

Yes. You must have property owner permission before searching. For public areas, follow local rules.

How long does a search take?

Most recoveries take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on conditions, target size, and search area.

How does pricing work?

Lost item recovery is on a Reward Basis. You pay what it's worth to you and what you can afford for me to come out and search for your lost item. For larger distance travel or very large search areas, we may confirm expectations up front.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Southeast Nebraska and Southwest Iowa. Travel outside the core area may be available by request.

Can you find property pins?

I can help locate and pinpoint metal markers and likely pin locations. This is not a legal survey. For official boundaries, hire a licensed surveyor.
