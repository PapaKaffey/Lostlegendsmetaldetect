FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need permission?

Yes. For private property, permission from the owner or manager is required before any search starts.

How long does a search take?

Most searches run 30 to 120 minutes, depending on property size, how precise the loss location is, and ground conditions.

How pricing works?

Pricing starts with a base service rate, then adjusts for travel distance, search area size, and site complexity.

What is no find, no fee?

For eligible lost-item recoveries, if the item is not recovered, labor is not charged. Travel or special access costs may still apply when disclosed up front.

What areas do you serve?

Lost Legends serves Southeast Nebraska and Southwest Iowa, including Omaha, Plattsmouth, Bellevue, Papillion, Nebraska City, Glenwood, and nearby communities.
