Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need permission?

Yes. You must have property owner permission before searching. For public areas, follow local rules.

How long does a search take?

Most recoveries take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on conditions, target size, and search area.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on travel distance, search size, and complexity. Some searches qualify for no find, no fee.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Southeast Nebraska and Southwest Iowa. Travel outside the core area may be available by request.

What is no find, no fee?

For eligible recoveries, you only pay if the item is found. Eligibility depends on location, conditions, and scope.
